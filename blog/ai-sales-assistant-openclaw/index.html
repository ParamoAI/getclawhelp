Common Pitfalls to Avoid

AI sales automation is powerful, but it can backfire if misused:

  • Over-automation of relationship building. AI handles research, drafting, and admin. But the actual relationship ‚Äî the trust, the rapport, the negotiation ‚Äî that's still human work. Never let AI send emails without review for high-value prospects.
  • Spray-and-pray at scale. The point isn't to email 10,000 people with AI-generated messages. It's to email 100 well-researched, perfectly-targeted prospects with messages that feel personal because they are.
  • Ignoring deliverability. AI can generate unlimited emails, but your domain reputation can't handle unlimited volume. Warm up gradually, monitor bounce rates, and respect sending limits.
  • No human review loop. Always review AI-generated outreach before it goes out, especially in the beginning. The AI will occasionally get tone wrong, include outdated info, or miss nuance. Your review catches these.
  • Forgetting to update context. Your product changes, your positioning evolves, your case studies grow. Keep the AI's knowledge current, or its outreach becomes stale.
