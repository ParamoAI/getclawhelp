Frequently Asked Questions

Does OpenClaw integrate with Shopify / WooCommerce?

Yes. OpenClaw connects to Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, and other platforms via their APIs. It can access order data, product catalogs, inventory levels, and customer information.

Can OpenClaw handle high-volume periods like Black Friday?

Yes. Since OpenClaw runs on your infrastructure, you can scale your server resources for peak periods. It handles thousands of concurrent conversations without degradation ‚Äî no waiting queues for customers.

How does OpenClaw handle payment or refund issues?

OpenClaw can initiate refunds through your payment processor's API (Stripe, PayPal, etc.) based on your configured rules. For complex payment disputes, it escalates to your team with full context.
