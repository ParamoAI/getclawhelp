How OpenClaw Solves This

Automated Appointment Scheduling

Frequently Asked Questions

Is OpenClaw HIPAA compliant?

OpenClaw runs on your own infrastructure, giving you full control over data security and compliance. For HIPAA compliance, deploy it on a HIPAA-eligible hosting environment (like AWS with a BAA), implement proper encryption, access controls, and audit logging. We help configure this during setup.

Can OpenClaw integrate with our EHR/EMR system?

Yes. OpenClaw can connect to EHR systems that offer APIs (Epic, Cerner, Athenahealth, DrChrono, etc.). Integration scope depends on your EHR's API capabilities and your organization's IT policies.

Does OpenClaw provide medical advice to patients?

No. OpenClaw is strictly an administrative assistant. It handles scheduling, reminders, intake, and operational tasks. It's configured to direct any clinical questions to your clinical staff. It does not diagnose, recommend treatments, or provide medical advice.
